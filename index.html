About Me

I was raised 60 miles north of New York City along the banks of the Hudson River in Newburgh, NY which is a small city of roughly 30,000 people. Being a home to our first president George Washington, Newburgh was one of the first great industrialized small cities in the United States.

Unfortunately, as great as Newburgh was back then, I didn't get to experience the rewards of it's golden years because of decades of political corruption, gentrification, poverty, and structural degradation of most of it's great historic buildings.
